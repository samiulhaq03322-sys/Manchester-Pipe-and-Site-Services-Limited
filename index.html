ESTABLISHED 2008

A HERITAGE OF QUALITY

Manchester Pipe and Site Services Limited was founded with a singular mission: to provide world-class infrastructure solutions to the North West's booming industrial sector.

Over the last decade, we have expanded our reach nationwide, becoming the primary choice for specialist pipeline installation and civil works. Our team comprises some of the most experienced site managers and engineers in the industry.

2008

Launched in Manchester with a focus on local drainage repair.

2015

Expanded into nationwide pipeline installation for the energy sector.

2023

Acquired major civil works contracts for regional airport expansion.
